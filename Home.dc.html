Our Story

A small shop with a long memory.

Maheshwaram began on a quiet lane in Mathura in 2012 — one wooden cabinet of fountain pens and a promise: stock only what we'd happily keep on our own desks.

Fourteen years on, we still test every nib, fold every gift, and know our regulars by the ink they prefer.
