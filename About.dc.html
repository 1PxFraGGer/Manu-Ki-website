Our Story

A small shop with a long memory.

Maheshwaram began in 2012 on a quiet lane in Mathura — one wooden cabinet of fountain pens and a single promise: stock only what we'd happily keep on our own desks.

Fourteen years on, that cabinet has become a much-loved shop — but the promise hasn't moved an inch.

How We Grew

One cabinet at a time

2012

The first cabinet

We opened with a single glass cabinet of fountain pens and a fierce belief that good tools make better writers.

2016

The nib bar arrives

We built a little counter where you could test any nib before buying. It's still the heart of the shop.

2020

Paper, art & gifting

We grew into journals, watercolours and letterpress cards — and started wrapping gifts the way we'd want ours wrapped.

2026

Still on the same lane

Bigger shelves, the same hands. We still test every nib, fold every gift, and know our regulars by their ink.

What We Believe

Three things we never compromise

Quality over quantity

We'd rather stock fifty pens we love than five hundred we don't. Everything here has earned its shelf.

Advice, freely given

Tell us what bleeds, what skips, what you're writing. Our staff actually use this stuff — and they'll tell you the truth.

Made to be kept

From refillable pens to hand-stitched journals, we choose things that last — and wrap them like they matter.

"We don't sell stationery. We help people find the pen they'll write their life with."

Rohan Maheshwari · Founder
